Chicken soup recipe

Chicke soup recipe

Description

Chicken soup is a classic comfort food made by simmering chicken with vegetables and noodles in a rich, flavorful broth. It's warm, hearty, and perfect for cold days. This recipe produces a wholesome and delicious soup that the whole family will love. It's simple to make and uses everyday ingredients you likely already have at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 1.5 kg)
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 6 cups water or chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnis

Steps

  1. Place the chicken in a large pot and cover with water or broth.
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to a simmer.
  3. Add onion, garlic, carrots, and celery to the pot.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 45–60 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Remove the chicken, shred the meat, and discard the bones.
  7. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.
  8. Add egg noodles and cook for another 8–10 minutes.
  9. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve hot garnished with fresh parsle
