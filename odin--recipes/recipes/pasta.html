Pasta Recipe

Description

Spaghetti pasta is one of the most beloved Italian dishes worldwide. Tender spaghetti noodles are tossed in a rich tomato-based meat sauce, making it a filling and satisfying meal. This classic recipe is quick to prepare and perfect for a weeknight dinner. Top it with freshly grated Parmesan cheese for the ultimate experience.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 300g ground beef or pork
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add onion and garlic, sauté until softened (about 3 minutes).
  4. Add ground meat and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
  5. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and stir well.
  6. Add oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  7. Simmer the sauce on low heat for 15–20 minutes.
  8. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the sauce.
  9. Toss well to combine and serve topped with Parmesan cheese.
