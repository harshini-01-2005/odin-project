pancakes recipe

Description

These fluffy pancakes are the perfect breakfast treat — golden on the outside, soft and airy on the inside. Made with simple pantry staples, they come together in just minutes. Serve them stacked high with butter and maple syrup, or top with fresh fruits and whipped cream for a special morning treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Butter or oil for the pan
  • Maple syrup for serving

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix — lumps are okay!
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter per pancake onto the pan.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and edges look set (about 2 minutes).
  7. Flip and cook the other side for 1–2 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Repeat with remaining batter.
  9. Serve warm with maple syrup and your favourite toppings
